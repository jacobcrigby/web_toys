# MICROCOSM

A small pond of evolving minds, in one self-contained HTML file. No libraries, no build step.

**Run it:** open `index.html` in a browser. That's all.

## What it is

Every mote in the dish steers by a tiny neural network (9 sensors → 8 hidden → turn/thrust)
that it inherited, mutated, from its parent. Nothing is scripted: motes that find food
reproduce, motes that don't starve, and behavior visibly improves over generations.

- **Plants** grow in drifting fertile patches; starved corpses rot back into detritus.
- A heritable **diet gene** makes flesh worth more and plants worth less — carnivore
  lineages can emerge on their own (watch the *mean diet* chart, and look for hot red cores).
- **Body genes** trade off: size vs. speed, sense radius vs. metabolic cost.
- Lineage **hue** is heritable, so speciation shows up as color clusters.

## Playing god

- Click open water to drop food; click a mote to put it under the lens
  (live brain activations included).
- **Feast** rains food. **Cataclysm** kills 85% — a bottleneck event.
- Sliders control mutation intensity and food abundance. Starve them and see what evolves.
